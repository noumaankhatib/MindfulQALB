Common Questions

Who can benefit from therapy?
Anyone going through emotional challenges, life transitions, relationship difficulties, anxiety, stress, or simply seeking personal growth can benefit from therapy. You don't need to be in crisis to seek support.
Is online therapy effective?
Yes! Research shows online therapy is as effective as in-person sessions for many concerns. It offers convenience, accessibility, and the comfort of your own space.
How long is each session?
Chat sessions are 45 minutes, audio consultations are 50 minutes, video consultations are 60 minutes, and couple therapy sessions run 90 minutes.
Is therapy confidential?
Absolutely. All sessions are strictly confidential, following APA ethical guidelines and Indian mental health tele-practice standards. Your privacy is paramount.
Do you work with NRI / international clients?
Yes! I work with Indians abroad across different time zones. Sessions are conducted online, making it accessible wherever you are - including clients from Europe, USA, Middle East and more.
What happens in the first session?
The first session is about getting to know you, understanding your concerns, and discussing your goals. It's a collaborative space where we'll determine the best approach for your journey. A free 10-minute introductory call is also available beforehand.
